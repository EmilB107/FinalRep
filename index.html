Where Strength Meets Gravity

10/4-5/2025

SWH, Halle-Saale, (Germany)

Streetlifting Worlds 2025

Streetlifting competitions is a premier powerlifting-style competition that blends the art of weighted calisthenics with raw strength. Athletes from around the globe will showcase their mastery in dips, pull-ups, squats, and muscle-ups, all performed with added weight.

Whether you're a seasoned street athlete, an enthusiast of calisthenics and powerlifting, or simply a fan of human strength and endurance, this event promises to inspire and awe.

Witness incredible feats of strength, connect with the global streetlifting community, and celebrate the evolution of calisthenics as a competitive sport.
